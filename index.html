Our Mission

Neighbor Seeker addresses a fundamental problem with how real estate transactions have historically been carried out. For too long the process has ignored the people with the biggest long term interest in the sale of any house -- the neighbors!

We've built a platform that allows neighbors to take a more proactive approach when a house in their neighborhood goes up for sale. No longer do they have to sit passively by and watch, wonder, and wait as prospective buyers are paraded through the house next door. With Neighbor Seeker they can influence who moves into that house by educating potential buyers about what makes their neighborhood great and what their expectations are for their new neighbor.

Simple put, at Neighbor Seeker our mission is to make neighborhoods better for everyone.
